TROOP LEVEL AND FORTIFICATION

  • level 2 troops are twice as effective as level 1 troops (if you have just specialised your troops to stealth or warrior your troops are still level 1 !)

  • level 3 troops are thice as effective as level 1 troops

  • level 4 troops are 4X as effective as level 1 troops. and well you see where it's going right upto level 8

  • before attacking all troops are normalised to level 1 from the above pattern

  • A level 2 fortified settlement will halve ( or make 1/2 of) the normalised soldiers

  • A level 3 fortified settlement will make 1/3 of the normalised soldiers

  • A level 4 fortified settlement will make 1/4 the normalised soldiers

  • A level 5 fortified settlement will make 1/5 the normalised soldiers

  • So attacking a settlement with fortification of 1 with 20 ,level 1 troops is mundane

  • Attacking a settlement with fortifification of 1 with 20 ,level 2 troops is like using 40 level 2 troops on the settlement

  • Attacking a settlement with fortifification of 2 with 20 ,level 2 troops is like using 20 level 2 troops on the settlement.again mundane
