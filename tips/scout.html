MOVING TIPS

  • If you move your troops to an occupied tile and do not settle, They will attack any enemy troops that try to move in at that tile It's a good defence in a way if the player doesn't scout before moving.That's why you could research open battle perk to hide some or all of your soldiers from scouting

  • Hidden soldiers are twice as effective as your troops who aren't.Troops automatically hide themselves when you move them. The percentage of soldiers who hide is based on your open battle perk

  • So it's your decision.Is the tile defended better with a settlement or just troops? and also if you could afford to lose 10% of all your resources to a possible attack later.
