TIPS AND TRICKS

  • you can attack ANY tile(as long as you can settle there if you win) you want.Even if the path to your target tile doesn't make sense.

  • Always scout the tile you want to attack , before you attack. Scouting an enemy tile after selecting troops gives you the probability of winning an attack on that tile.Basically keep your win probability >50% otherwise the mini-game would be made harder for you.On the other hand if you attack with win probability >90% you would progressively get easier experience in the mini-game as the probabilty increases over 90% and exact opposite when win probability is below 50%

  • the amount of troops you lose in an attack is the percentage of health of your sprite at the end of the attack mini-game so quality of play counts! you play as :

    • archer - if you have selected stealth troop in research or haven't upgraded your troops at all
    • swordsman - if you have selected warrior troops in research

  • Bored of the attack mini game or is it too hard? You could use the sim_attack option so your attack result would be calculated based on your win probability and the losses will be based on your research and win probability.

  • Each tile has a contribution to your resource regeneration.So if you lose a tile you lose the regen contribution from that tile and 10% of your resrouces as plunder to the attacker.
